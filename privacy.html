Privacy Policy

last_updated: 2026-02-26

This policy describes how BadGameDeveloper handles information in connection with our games, including Unpropable.

1. Information We Collect

Our games use Epic Online Services (EOS) for multiplayer. When you play, the following is processed:

  • Your Epic Games display name
  • Your Epic Account ID (session management & auth)
  • Gameplay session data (lobbies, matchmaking, invites)
  • Basic device info for connectivity

2. How We Use It

Solely to provide multiplayer functionality:

  • Hosting and joining lobbies
  • Matchmaking and session management
  • Social features (friend invites via Epic overlay)
  • Displaying your in-game username

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information. Session data flows through Epic Games' EOS infrastructure under their terms.

4. Storage & Retention

We don't run user data servers. Account data is managed by Epic Games. Local settings (username, graphics) are stored on your device only.

5. Children's Privacy

Our games aren't directed at children under 13. Epic Games handles age-related restrictions through their platform.

6. Your Rights

Manage your data through your Epic Games account settings.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes are reflected here with an updated date.

8. Contact

Questions? Reach out via GitHub.
