Unpropable — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Unpropable uses Epic Online Services (EOS) for multiplayer functionality. When you play, the following information is processed:

  • Your Epic Games display name
  • Your Epic Account ID (for session management)
  • Gameplay session data (lobbies, matchmaking)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to provide multiplayer game functionality, including hosting lobbies, matchmaking, and the social overlay (friend invites).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Game session data is processed through Epic Online Services infrastructure.

4. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. All account data is managed by Epic Games through their EOS platform.

5. Your Rights

You may manage your Epic Games account and data through your Epic Games account settings.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out via our GitHub repository.
